    here's how to play

    step one: turn off your self view, here’s how in zoom:


    go to your video and click the button with the three dots in the upper right hand corner. if you don’t see it immediately, hover for it to appear


    a menu will drop down. scroll to the bottom and select hide self view



    step two: have someone share a screen


    in the lower panel of your zoom screen there is a green button that says "share screen" - click that!



    step three: make your person larger than life :)


    hover in the blank space by the video until a grey bar appears



    drag the line until you see your person’s video get larger


    almost ready, remember to...

    • consider brushing up on reducing zoom fatigue
    • check in with your person & make sure they are in the headspace to play (we'll leave this one up to you)
    • figure out a system that works for skipping a card (we suggest a safe word)
    • have fun, feel free to finish the game by contributing: submit a question
